Delete Your Account & Data

To permanently delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Open the LIFTY app
  2. Go to Settings → Account
  3. Tap "Delete Account" and confirm

If you cannot access the app, email support@lifty.app with your registered email address and "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line. We will process your request within 48 hours.

Export Your Data

You can export your workout history and fitness data at any time:

  1. Open the LIFTY app
  2. Go to Settings → Data & Privacy
  3. Tap "Export My Data"

Your data will be sent to your registered email as a CSV or JSON file.
