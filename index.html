Delete Your Account & Data

To permanently delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Open the VolumeLogic app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap "Delete Account" and confirm

If you cannot access the app, email support@volumelogic.se with your registered email address and "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line. We will process your request within 48 hours.
