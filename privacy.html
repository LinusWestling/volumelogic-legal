Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 14, 2026

LIFTY ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

We collect your email address for account creation and synchronization. We also store your workout logs (exercises, sets, weights) to provide the app's core functionality.

We use Supabase for secure data storage and RevenueCat for processing subscriptions. We do not sell your data to advertisers.

You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the app settings at any time.
